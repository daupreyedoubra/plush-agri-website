\# FRONTEND DESIGN SKILL — PLUSH AGRI SOLUTIONS



\## Design Philosophy

\- Minimalist. Editorial. Premium. Quiet confidence.

\- Inspired by: World Bank, One Acre Fund, McKinsey Impact, high-end NGO websites

\- Every section must have generous breathing room

\- Large imagery. Large white space. Grid-based layout

\- Nothing should feel crowded or startup-like



\## Color Tokens

\- --color-primary: #1F4D36 (Field Authority — dominant green)

\- --color-secondary: #5E8B3D (Growth Green)

\- --color-trust: #163A70 (Veterinary Trust — navy blue)

\- --color-accent: #DCE8D7 (Soft Sage)

\- --color-neutral: #F6F2E8 (Cream Neutral)

\- --color-white: #FFFFFF



\### Approved Combinations Only

\- Field Authority on Cream

\- Growth Green on White

\- White on Field Authority

\- White on Veterinary Trust

\- Field Authority on Soft Sage



\### Never Use

\- Lime green, bright blue, neon colors, random gradients



\## Typography

\- Font: Poppins (Google Fonts)

\- Display: Poppins Bold — hero headlines

\- Heading: Poppins Bold — section titles

\- Body: Poppins Regular — paragraphs, descriptions

\- Accent: Poppins Italic — Hausa translations, taglines

\- Never mix fonts. Never use system fonts.



\## Spacing System

\- Base unit: 8px

\- Section padding: minimum 80px top and bottom (100px on desktop)

\- Component gap: 24px minimum

\- Never cram content — always default to more space



\## Layout Rules

\- Max content width: 1280px centered

\- 12-column grid on desktop

\- Single column on mobile

\- Large editorial images — never small thumbnails

\- Use asymmetric layouts for visual interest



\## Component Patterns



\### Buttons

\- Primary: White text on Field Authority (#1F4D36), 48px height, 0px border-radius (sharp, not pill)

\- Secondary: Field Authority text, transparent background, 2px Field Authority border

\- Hover: slight darken on primary, fill on secondary

\- Never rounded pill buttons — too startup-like



\### Cards

\- Background: White or Cream Neutral

\- Border: 1px solid #DCE8D7

\- Padding: 32px

\- No drop shadows — use border instead

\- No rounded corners beyond 4px



\### Navigation

\- Sticky, background: White with bottom border in Soft Sage

\- Logo left, links center or right

\- Single CTA button: "Talk to a Vet" in Field Authority



\### Forms

\- Clean, minimal — label above input

\- Input border: 1px solid #DCE8D7

\- Focus state: Field Authority border color

\- No floating labels



\## Animation Rules (Framer Motion)

\### Allowed

\- fade-up on section entry (y: 30 to 0, opacity 0 to 1)

\- fade-in on images

\- staggered children reveals (0.1s delay per item)

\- hover elevation (scale: 1.02, transition: 0.2s)

\- counter animations on impact numbers

\- parallax under 5% on hero images



\### Never Use

\- Spin animations

\- Flying objects or floating blobs

\- Glassmorphism effects

\- Particle effects

\- Bouncing or elastic easing



\## Photography Direction

\- Authentic Nigerian farming imagery only

\- Northern Nigerian livestock farmers, real farm visits

\- Veterinarians in the field, training sessions

\- Never: western farming, tractors in wheat fields, lab coats in sterile rooms

\- Use Unsplash with queries: "nigeria farmer", "africa livestock", "cattle northern nigeria"

\- Images must feel documented, not staged



\## What To Avoid

\- Generic AI aesthetic (floating cards, random gradients, glass effects)

\- Startup design patterns (neon, dark mode defaults, big emoji)

\- Overly rounded corners

\- Drop shadows on everything

\- Icon-heavy layouts

\- Dashboard visuals

\- Testimonial carousels with star ratings
